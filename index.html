Come join us on our first tour to Kalbarri. 2 Days for $200 including food ( 2 lunches and 1 Dinner ) Limited spots available. English and Mandarin spoken on the tour.

On the 12th and 13th of January covering 1200km of the Western Australian Coast.

We will stay in a dorm at the Kalbarri Backpackers.

We will see:

  • Greenough Wildlife & Bird Park
  • Geraldton Sydney Memorial
  • Pink Lakes
  • Sunset on Red Bluff
  • Kalbarri National Park (Natures Window)
  • Central Greenough Historic village
  • Pinnacles for the Night Stars
  • Leaning Tree

What to bring:

  • A Water Bottle
  • Clothes for one night and something warm for the night events
  • Good Walking shoes
  • Hat and Sunglasses
  • Toilet Bag

Please keep the luggage to a backpack size as space is limited on the bus.
